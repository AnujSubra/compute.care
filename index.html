Igniting Young Minds Through Technology

Our goal is to launch a foundational computer science program for approximately 50 talented and underprivileged students in grades 6 through 8 in Panayapatti, a small rural village in the Pudukkottai district of Tamil Nadu, starting in September 2025. This initiative aims to bridge the digital divide by providing essential technology skills, fostering innovation, and opening doors to future opportunities in the digital world. We believe that empowering these young minds with technology can create a lasting positive impact on their lives and the community.

What we do

Education

Key Learning Areas for students: Computer Fundamentals: Understanding hardware, operating systems, and safe internet practices. Introduction to Programming: Learning the fundamentals of coding using Python, a versatile and beginner-friendly language. Project-Based Learning: Applying learned concepts to create a small, functional application.

Resources

Help with compute grants, tooling recommendations, and cost-effective infrastructure.

Events

Workshops, hackathons, and seminars focused on reproducible computation and open science.
