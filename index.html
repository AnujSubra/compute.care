Igniting Young Minds Through Technology

Talent is everywhere, but opportunity isn't. In the villages of Tamil Nadu, bright high school students are being left behind in the digital age.

The Compute Initiative works to close this gap by providing computers, internet access, and guidance to students who need them most.

About The First Compute Initiative: SRM Arunachalam Higher Secondary School, Panayapatti

The Compute Initiative (TCi) has partnered with a government-aided SRM Arunachalam Higher Secondary School in Panayapatti (Pudukottai District) to empower rural young students by introducing Computer Science learning opportunities and resources.
