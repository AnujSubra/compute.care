About The Compute Initiative

The Compute Initiative is a community-driven organization focused on improving access to compute resources, training, and best practices for researchers and civic technologists. We run events, mentorship programs, and help teams secure cost-effective compute.

Our mission

To democratize access to computational resources so that research and public-interest projects can thrive without prohibitive infrastructure costs or barriers.
