About The Compute Initiative

The Compute Initiative (TCI) is a nonprofit bringing computer science education to under-resourced government-aided schools in rural Tamil Nadu, India — a partnership between TCI, our partner schools, and the local communities of Panayapatti and Kuruvikondanpatti.

Our mission

Talent is everywhere, but opportunity isn't. In the villages of Tamil Nadu, bright students in grades 6–9 are being left behind in the digital age. TCI provides daily computer science instruction and teacher training, bringing technology access directly into students' hands and connecting them with mentors who can guide them. It's about more than skills — it's about showing these students they have the power to create, dream bigger, and open doors they never knew existed. We mobilize volunteers through grassroots recruiting, fundraise, and run every part of the operation ourselves: hardware, internet installation, and school logistics, so the benefits of the digital age reach students who'd otherwise go without them.
