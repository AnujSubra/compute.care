Events

The Compute Institute (TCi) is proud to announce the inauguration of a new AI-enabled computer lab, a community initiative led by its founders, Anish Subramanian and Anuj Subramanian.

We are honored to have the Honorable Tamil Nadu Minister for Natural Resources, Mr. S. Ragupathi, officiate the ceremony. He will be joined by local educators, dignitaries, and community leaders.

This initiative aims to empower rural students by introducing Computer Science as a standard subject for grades 6 through 10. TCi will donate computers and provide ongoing support with a dedicated curriculum and training.
